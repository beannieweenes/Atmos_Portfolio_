01 — Dossier

Product Designer based in Jaipur. Four years across enterprise, government, and healthcare — Capgemini trained, freelancing now.

I care most about the parts of design other people skip: the error states, the edge cases, the handoff spec that keeps the build honest. The work should hold up when something goes wrong, not just when everything goes right.

The space theme on this portfolio isn't accidental. I've always been drawn to the idea that the best interfaces are like good spacecraft design — everything stripped back to what's essential, nothing wasted, and it still has to work flawlessly when the stakes are highest. That's the standard I hold my work to.

When I'm not designing I'm gaming, making playlists, or trying to figure out music production. I've recently added "learning to use a DAW" to the list of things I'm bad at but refuse to stop doing.
